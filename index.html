Characters Summary Review

Zarvora

An ambitious, intelligent, and rutheless woman with a mission.

She has built her own manually operated computer and has plans to use it to become the most powerful person in the known world and stop dooms day.

Deadly with a rifle, she has won several duels and isn't afraid to destroy anyone who gets in her way.

Lemorel

A smart, capable, and deadly lady looking to escape her past.

She has had and won several duels which, amongst other things, led to her becoming a black sheep in her town.

She is guarded, not very sociable, but is fairly gifted in mathematics, a skill set the new highlibre has high demand for.

Glaskin

Glaskin is a intelligent, selfish, womaniser who frequently commits petty crime.

Hes strong, confident, and can think quick on his feet.

Dolorian

A lower ranked but very attractive member of of Libre Dragons.

She befriends Lemorel who teaches her to shoot. She teaches Lemorel about love life.

Shes interested in living the easy life, dressing up and going to dinner parties, flirting with guys. But shes loyal to her friends.

Darien

Darien is a mute Dragon Blue Linguist with no real friends until Lemorel came along.

Darien was so excited to finally have somebody she could speak sign language to she insisted Lemorel stay at her place and they quickly became best friends.

Abbess

A northern realms princess who can resist the call. Shes nice but use her status to her advantage.

Has a big interest in discovering the calls source and unravaling that mystery.

She has taught her devoted brother Ilyire to also resist the call.

Souls in the Great Machine

Chapter 1 - Champions

Zarvora is busy solidifying her controversial position as new highlibre by winning duels to the death and other means of forcing people on board with her radical new direction.

She simply humiliated the grand games master by having her Computer, the only one in existance and operated by humans, beat him at his own game, champions.

This Calculor is essential to her plans to make Rochester, and herself the center of power in the known world.

Chapter 2 - Captial

Lemorel sees a way out of her town and troubled past and is desperate to take it, leaving on the first cycle powered train out of there.

She manages to get promoted and hired to work in Rochester at the main library, the same one Zarvora and her human computer is.

She befriends Darien, a mute Dragon Blue linguist and settles into her new life.

Chapter 3 - Courtship

An assination attempt on Zarvora fails but the second in command of the library Lewick is killed.

Tarrin, the new second in command, is next but survives, the suspicous members of the library are rounded up and forced to work as prisoners / components on the great computer.

Lemorel meets Glaskin and they become somewhat secret lovers.

Chapter 4 - Captive

Darien is promoted and give the responsibility of oversight of the train and beam station expansion, to the benefit of Rochester.

Upon overseeing expansion of a distant outpost in the far north, warriors from a civilization across the desert, and beyond the known world, ambush Darien and the rest in the middle of a Call.

They are on a mission serving the Abbess and the man covered in a living tree like ghille suit is the one who can avoid the call.

The rest are tortured and killed but the Ghillie suit man lets Darien go as it is what the Abbess would want.

Chapter 5 - Coding

Lemorel gains control of the Calculor behind Zarvoras back. She uses it to uncover tampered comunications coming from an eastern district. She manages to unveil the group with help from a genius code breaker operating the repeater. This code breaker accidentally threatens to reveal to Zarvora that Lemorel had used the calculor behind her back, which would mean death for Lemorel, so she throws him under the bus to save herself.

Lemorel also used the Calculor to find out that Glaskin was cheating on her with multiple other women and was lying to and using her to get off with crimes. She uses her powers to have him prisoner for life as a component in the Calculor.

Chapter 6 - Call

The vine man is captured and Darien visits him, still pretending to not understand his language he divulges his escapades over the last couple of months.

He found the source of the call, it was at the ocean and animals would leap off the cliffs to feed the sea creatures.

She let him go free back to his master Abbess but with a little addendum added to her note. She implored the Abbess to come back south to her land and join the Dragon Libres were women could be truely free, unlike in her culture.

Chapter 7 - Coup

Zarvora is pushing for war with a much more powerful state, the Mayor Jefton stands his grounds and challenges her to a duel.

The Mayors Champion is bested by Zarvora however the judges are split on whether she cheated so shes sent to jail for 11 months.

Tarrin, the Calculors second in command, is struggling to maintain its operation. Rochester goes massively downhill due to its reliance on the Calculor.

Lemorel steps up and offers to take charge. She struggles with the Calculor but does a better job than Tarrin. They both conspire against one of the judges who voted against Zarvora to frame him.

The judge is disgraced Zarvora is freed and she becomes friendly with Lemorel after seeing her potential with the machine.

Chapter 8 - Combat

Zarvora activates her plan to to attack the much larger Tandarin using her portable battle calculor.

Glaskin and Nikalan are selected as the 2 main components of the battle Calculor and training exercises proves it to be quite useful, managing to upset opposite forces of up to x8.

The real battle against the larger force is a massive success but in the chaos Glaskin and Nikalan escape.

They are captured by slave traders and then Glaskin is captured by Ilyire and the Abbess as they march south to the source of the call, and to the dragon Libres.

Chapter 9 - Catastrophy

The Abbess meets Zarvora who reveals she also can resist the call and she thinks its to do with bird genes or hair samples. They find a place were many people can resist it and head there.

Lemorel leaves with Gaskin to travel north to Glenellen to find Nikalan. Glaskin is supposed to be her guide but soon escapes so she kills some northerner and forces him to be her new guide.

Chapter 10 - Chrysalis

Zarvora becomes pregnant and suddenly promotes her slave lover Darkin to her husband, much to his shock.

Glaskin has been living with Monks for the last 5 years waiting for his chance to escape. He does so, assumes a new name and becomes a navvy.

Ilyire is destroyed mentally after his partner Darien leaves him and his previously loyal, playful sister, now cold and calculating, shoots him possibly to kill. They both reject him for sticking to his old jealus, religous ways.

Chapter 11 - Cantrip

Lemorel has been busy forming an army of bandits and attacks Glenellens Battle Calculor assisted army by messing with the Calculor and other cunning tactics. She aims to become ruler of Glenellen and all the northern territories.

Ilyire attacks Glaskin and is defeated, Glaskin shows sympathy to the broken man and takes him out drinking and whoring.

Zarvora learns of Glakins reapearance and informs the Abbess. She has also begun work on an electrically powered Calculor made possible in a huge faraday cage the mayor of Kalwoorgie has been working on.

Abbess Therelsa lures Glaskin to the edge to preform more experiments involving the Call but Glaskin reveals hes already learned to become resistant to it from his 5 years of monk training. Theresla leaves but Glaskin heads back to the edge for Ilyire and the treasure.

Chapter 12 - Clocksmith

Glaskin uses his riches to buy a Tavern and falls for, unbenownst to him, Lemorels tall sister. This is quickly interrupted however as Zarvora discovers his presence and recruits him to work with her new calculor.

Lemorel finally meets Nikalan however discovers he is a shell of the man he once was. Murdering the man that took all the credit for making the Glenellen Calculor, she releases Nikalan and gives him money and power but no longer feels love for him. Her sights now set on the Southern Territories, figuring Zarvora plans to move North to capture her lands.

Chapter 13 - Chaos

Lemorels Neverlanders cross the desert in a suprise attack on Zarvora, the Overmayor. Zarvora is in the middle of testing her rockets, one of which hit the sun shield when this happened. She just barely manages to escape with her life.

Lemorels Neverlanders manages to capture much territory at the start, with help from the spy merchants she sent before. Her attacks focused on infrastructure and using it for her own ends.

The battle lines settle down and gains become hard fought. Glaskin joins Zarvoras army.

Chapter 14 - Casualty

The 6 AI controlled orbital fortresses are triggered into battle against the mirror sun AI. Mirrorsun is heavily damaged but is victorious meaning their are no more fortresses to blast EMPs at any electronics they detect.

Zarvora discovers this and immediately begins building sparkflash radio communication devices and deploys them to gain the upper hand on the battle field. Somebody from another uknown contenent, the USA, hears the transmissions and begins transmitting back although they have trouble understanding each other.

Glaskin repairs old wounds with Dolorian after she teased him when he was prisoner under direction of her old friend Lemorel. They meet on the battlefield however become surrounded.

Dolorian uses the sparkflash to send a plea for help to Zarvora Cybeline. The mirrorsun interprets this request and saves them by scortching the Neverlander forces.

This brings Lemorel to negotiate and Glaskin baits her into dueling with Dolorian.

Lemorel knows she can easily beat her, she taught her what she knows, however Dolorian picks a long range, crouches down then stands just before Lemorels bullet hits her. Lemorel walks away thinking shes dead but with her last breathe she shoot Lemorel dead.

Chapter 15 - Conversion

The war is now pretty much over. Just a few holdouts remain including Tarrin at Rochester who was allied to Lemorel.

Glaskin and Ilyire together with help from the Aviads / Dolphins take over Rochester with the help of a call. Tarrin is executed and the war is over at last.

The Aviads plan a voyage to the Americas, they also wish to destroy Zarvora and the humans.

Time skips forwards a decade and life kind of goes back to normal.

Zarvora manages to communicate with the mirror sun and she gets sent a bracelet that allows the mirrorsun to experience life through her. She says "This is the second time I've become the first soul in a great machine."

Review

Souls in the Great Machine was very enjoyable and was well worth the read for me. I can see why it isn't considered one of the all time classics but is well respected in its nieche.

The world building is good, with all the different creations they come up with to get around limitations on electricity and steam and why the limitation are there in the first place. It's not to complex either. I also like how it restricts it's self to just the South end of Australia and the mysteries what lies beyond.

The characters are pretty good too. Not too many of them and each with memorable personalities and quirks.

I found the mystery of the plot and where its going to be intreuging and made me want to find out whats going to happen next.

I didn't really like how Lemorel became the Leader of the Neverlanders. In fact, I didn't much like the Neverlanders sections in general. I also thought the characters came off as to over powered and Mary Sues. Like Lemorel is an expert dueler, and manages to take over and control all of the Allspring armies even though shes not even from that culture. Zarvora is the worst for this, shes super inteligent, can build computers and lots of other new tech, has discovered how all these diferent AIs and mirror sun work, is an expert dueler and strategist, and becomes Overmayor from nothing. Having said that I do like her character, how shes not an out and out good guy and is somewhat socially awkward, doing awful things in what she considers the greater good.

Another thing I didn't like was how components of the Calculor loved it and were devoted to Zarvora despite what she'd done to them. Especially Denkar who becomes her husband.

Thats just being a bit nit picky however. Most of the plot is solid and interesting. I liked the world, the building of technologies, and the greater mysteries it introduces. I also think the idea of human components in a computer is cool. It also had some emotional moments it there.

It's a trillogy so I'm interested in seeing what happens with the next 2 books!
