Characters Summary Review

Zarvora

An ambitious, intelligent, and rutheless woman with a mission.

She has built her own manually operated computer and has plans to use it to become the most powerful person in the known world and stop dooms day.

Deadly with a rifle, she has won several duels and isn't afraid to destroy anyone who gets in her way.

Lemorel

A smart, capable, and deadly lady looking to escape her past.

She has had and won several duels which, amongst other things, led to her becoming a black sheep in her town.

She is guarded, not very sociable, but is fairly gifted in mathematics, a skill set the new highlibre has high demand for.

Chapter 1 - Champions

Zavora is busy solidifying her controversial position as new highlibre by winning duels to the death and other means of forcing people on board with her radical new direction.

She simply humiliated the grand games master by having her Computer, the only one in existance and operated by humans, beat him at his own game, champions.

This Calculor is essential to her plans to make Rochester, and herself the center of power in the known world.

Chapter 2 - Captial

Lemorel sees a way out of her town and troubled past and is desperate to take it, leaving on the first cycle powered train out of there.

She manages to get promoted and hired to work in Rochester at the main library, the same one Zavora and her human computer is.

She befriends Darien, a mute Dragon Blue linguist and settles into her new life.

Chapter 3 - Courtship

An assination attempt on Zavora fails but the second in command of the library Lewick is killed.

Tarrin, the new second in command, is next but survives, the suspicous members of the library are rounded up and forced to work as prisoners / components on the great computer.

Lemorel meets Glaskin and they become somewhat secret lovers.
